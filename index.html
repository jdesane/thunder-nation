Who We Are

Building Quality Citizens Through Baseball

The South Florida Thunder is a 501(c)(3) non-profit corporation with a focus on travel baseball and softball. Based out of North Palm Beach, Florida, we field 10 baseball teams and 1 softball team across age groups from 7U to 13U.

Our mindset is "we are stronger together than we are apart" — we encourage a connection between all teams throughout the organization, creating a community we call Thunder Nation.

Proud Partners of the Miami Marlins

The Thunder is proud to be powered by the Miami Marlins. Our goal is to build lifelong fans of baseball, starting with our home team.

  • Teams have played on the field at loanDepot Park
  • Players have thrown out the first pitch at Marlins games
  • Helped groundskeepers change bases between innings
  • Led the crowd with Billy the Marlin
Our Values

A Culture of Winning

We believe winning is a byproduct of hard work, dedication, teamwork, and commitment.

🏆

Excellence

We want our Thunder players to be winners — it's harder to win than to lose, and we never want complacency in our program.

🤝

Community

Thunder Nation events like our preseason Welcome at Roger Dean Stadium and Thundermania build lasting bonds across all teams.

⭐

Character

Our mission is to develop quality citizens through the sport of baseball — the best players on the field and in life.
