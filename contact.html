FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions



Tryouts are typically held in the fall (August/September) for the spring season and in December for the following year. Contact us for specific dates for your player's age group.
We have teams from 7U through 13U for baseball, plus a travel softball team. Multiple divisions (Black, Gold, Grey) are available at certain age groups.
Our teams compete in USSSA, Perfect Game, GSA (Global Sports Alliance), and the SFL Travel League. Tournament participation varies by age group and skill level.
Teams practice 2–3 times per week with tournaments most weekends. The main competitive season runs from spring through summer, with a fall ball season as well. Check our Schedule page for details.
Yes! The South Florida Thunder is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit corporation. Our mission is to develop quality citizens through the sport of baseball.
We have an official partnership with the Miami Marlins. This gives our players unique experiences like playing on the field at loanDepot Park, throwing out first pitches, and attending exclusive Marlins events.
